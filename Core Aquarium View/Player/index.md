# Player

In the Core Aquarium View, the player is simply a cursor.

# Controls

Escape = Open menu

Left-Clicking also bound to 'X'
Left-Clicking on Water/Air = Feeding Fish
Left-Clicking on Fish = Moves Fish slightly
Left-Clicking on Enemy = Damages Enemy depending on weapon
Left-Clicking on Coins = Collects coins

Right-Clicking = Nothing for now
