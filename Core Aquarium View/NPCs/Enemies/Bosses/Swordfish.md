# Sword Fish

15 HP
0 Mana
1 hp damage / second touch

## In-game Description

A blind fish with a pointy sword. He only wants to play tag.

## Traits

Damage on touch 1hp/sec, regardless if sword is touched or body is touched

## Movement Tendencies

Slowly swims in random directions.

## Attacks

Touch damage, no special attacks

## Drops on Death

1 large bill ($20)
