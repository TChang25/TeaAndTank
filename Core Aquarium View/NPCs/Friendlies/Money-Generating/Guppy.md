# Guppy

5 HP (+2 for each stage)
0 Mana

## In-game Description

A common fish. Creates copper coins. 

## Specialties

- Drops copper coins every 10 seconds. If fish is hungry, the coin timer is only paused, not reset.

## Movement Tendencies

- Moves incredibly slow.
- Can go up, down, diagonal.

## Growth Conditions

- Fish must be fed for 30 seconds. If fish is hungry, the timer for growing is only paused, not reset.

## Growth Stages

Guppy can grow into 1 larger guppy. Growing is represented by enlarging the fish.

### Inital stage

Drops 1 copper coin every coin timer 10 seconds. 

### 2nd Stage

Drops 2 copper coin every coin timer 15 seconds.
